About Us

Our founder, James Mitchell, started the company after 15 years of experience in luxury home construction, bringing together a team of the most talented architects, designers, and craftsmen in the region.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical home building timeline?

Our custom homes typically take 8-12 months to complete, depending on size and complexity. This includes 2-3 months for design and permitting, followed by 6-9 months of construction. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultation.

What areas do you serve in Raleigh?

We serve the entire Greater Raleigh area, including North Hills, Cary, Wake Forest, Apex, and surrounding communities within a 50-mile radius. Each area has specific building codes and requirements that we're well-versed in.

What is your warranty coverage?

We offer a comprehensive 10-year structural warranty, 5-year systems warranty, and 2-year workmanship warranty. All appliances and fixtures are covered by manufacturer warranties, which we help manage.

What is the typical cost per square foot?

Custom home costs typically range from $200-$400 per square foot, depending on finishes and features. We provide detailed cost breakdowns during the planning phase and work with you to optimize your budget.

Do you handle all permits and approvals?

Yes, we manage all necessary permits, inspections, and regulatory approvals. Our team has extensive experience with local building codes and maintains strong relationships with municipal authorities.
